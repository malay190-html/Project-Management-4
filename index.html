Visitor Parking Issues and Solutions

Problem Statement

Visitor parking issues at universities often stem from insufficient parking spaces, poor time management, and the lack of effective navigation systems. These challenges result in delays, frustration among visitors, and reduced overall efficiency.

Proposed Solutions

  • Increase parking spaces to accommodate more visitors.
  • Implement time management strategies to streamline parking usage.
  • Introduce digital parking management systems for real-time updates and reservations.
  • Promote alternatives such as public transportation and cycling to reduce parking demand.
  • Improve visitor experience with better signage and navigation aids.
